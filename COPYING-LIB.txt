
*libdb43.dll
a recompiled binary of officialy distributed source code of BerkelyDB by gonzui-win32 project.
The licence is BerkeleyDB's.

*bdb.so
gonzui-win32 distribution includes "ruby-bdb" -- a ruby binding of BerkeleyDB.
ruby-bdb is distribute at http://raa.ruby-lang.org/project/bdb.
The licence is Ruby's.
Binary distributeion for ruby-mswin32 is available at http://soutaro.com/ruby-bdb-win32/.
